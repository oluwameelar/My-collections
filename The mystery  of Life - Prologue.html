The mystery of Life -- SUMMARY


The sojourn onboard all begins with wailing and unconscious activities. The reason behind this is yet to be figured out. Then comes the stages which cannot be fathomed out. Lots of activities,designed but not planned, have been put in place to make the journey through it mesmerizing and obviously diverse for different entities. It smiles this minute and the next minute,it wears a frowning face. It comes more like a whip,going back and forth,leaving no hint of its next agenda. The best way to utilize its commodities is to never let a minute slide without making fruitful moves,innovative ideas and breakthrough plans, so as to keep sailing successfully on its ship. Then comes the shipwreck,rather unexpectedly,which signifies the end of one's sojourn in it,giving rise to the next adventurer to explore. Even at that,it never ends - it goes on and on.



Life,indeed,is a MYSTERY.
